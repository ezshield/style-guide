Corporate Logo Guidelines

EZShield's Corporate Logo

Get to know EZShield's official corporate brand logo and how to use it online and in print. Follow the sizing, space and placement guidelines below to ensure clear, high-quality image resolution, avoid distortion of the image and maintain excellent contrast between the image and its background.

For use on: www.EZShield.com, www.GoEZShield.com and EZShield Corporate Marketing.

Standard Full Color

Complete brand name in full color to be used everywhere as the default logo for representing EZShield.

Download .png for web Download .eps for print

Minimum Size

It is not permissible to use the logo less than 150 pixels wide (web) or 1.375 inches (print) to ensure a crisp legible image.
